SOME IMPORTANT PRECAUTIONS

STAY HOME

SAVE LIVES

1. Stay home if you feel unwell.

2. Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.

3. Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

4. Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.

5. Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.

6. If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention. Call in advance.

7. Follow the directions of your local health authority.
