MOST COMMON SYMPTOMS OF COVID-19

FEVER

If a person has a fever with a dry cough, they may have symptoms of COVID-19. ... or weeks with no explanation are called fevers of unknown origin.

***

DRY COUGH

An occasional cough is healthy, but one that persists for weeks, produces bloody mucus, causes changes in phlegm colour or comes with fever, dizziness or fatigue may be a sign you need to see a doctor.

***

TIREDNESS

the tiredness is often overwhelming and isn't relieved by sleep and rest.
